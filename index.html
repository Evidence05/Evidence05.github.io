About Me

I'm Ali, a cybersecurity student at UTS passionate about the intersection of cloud security, AI regulation, and evolving cyber threats. I explore how technical controls and policy frameworks can work in tandem to build resilient digital systems.


My work spans threat modelling, cloud infrastructure security, and the governance challenges that emerge as AI becomes embedded in critical systems. I'm driven by the belief that the best security professionals are both technical and policy-literate.
